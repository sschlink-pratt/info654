Mechanical Pencil: Uni Kuru Toga KS

The mechanical pencil Sam enjoys using the most is the Uni Kuru Toga KS 0.5mm. It costs about $7.

Perfect for taking notes in graduate school! This mechanical pencil is a Kuru Toga, which means it automatically rotates the lead inside, ensuring a consistent width as you write.

Sam has tried most of the Kuru Toga mechanical pencils at this point and likes this model the best.

Sam prefers Pentel Ain lead and Rhodia DotPad paper.
